To excel with the **legends battlegrounds dark lord**, you must move beyond spamming skills. High-level play involves "M1 weaving"—inserting basic attacks between skills to maximize damage output before the enemy's knockback recovery kicks in.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Opener">
    Approach with 3 basic M1 attacks. On the third hit, immediately cancel the animation into **Shadow Grasp** to pull the enemy back before they are knocked away.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Extension">
