<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Jun Combo Guide">

To maximize your efficiency with **legends battlegrounds jun**, you must learn to chain his moves without allowing the opponent a window to use their Ragdoll Cancel (Q).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Reliable Ground Opener">
    Start with a **Forward Dash** into a 3-hit M1 string. Immediately follow with a **Downslam**. While the opponent is grounded, activate **Fang Dance**. This confirms the full cinematic damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Glimpse Transition">
    After the Fang Dance knockback, perform a **Side Dash** to realign, then a **Forward Dash** into another 3-hit M1 string. Use **Glimpse of Power** and immediately press 4 to trigger the **Glimpse Cascade** variant.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Wall Conversion">
    If the opponent is near a wall, use an M1 knockback to trigger the **Wall Sequence**. As they are launched upward, use **Airborne Virgule Cascade** to finish the string with high vertical damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combo Optimization">

</Callout>
