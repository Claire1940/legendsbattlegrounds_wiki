<Section icon={Target} title="The Wolf Fang Flash Combo Extension">

Mastering the "Wolf Fang Flash Method" is what separates casual players from **legends battlegrounds kj** pros. This technique allows you to extend a combo beyond the standard limits by manipulating the dash portion of the move.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Positioning">
    Engage the opponent with a standard M1 string or a mini-uppercut to force them into a defensive stance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Backflip Angle">
    During the backflip part of *Wolf Fang Flash*, turn your character slightly (about 30 degrees). This prevents the dash from hitting the opponent directly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Sideways Dash">
    Intentionally miss the punch dash by dashing right to the side of the opponent. This places you in their blind spot while they are still in hitstun or blockstun.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Re-Engage">
    Immediately follow up with a new M1 string or a Variant. Because you didn't trigger the "finisher" animation of the dash, the combo counter doesn't reset as quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Consistency Tip">
Facing sideways during the backflip is much more reliable than trying to dash-cancel manually. Practice this in the training area until the 30-degree angle becomes muscle memory.
</Callout>

</Section>
