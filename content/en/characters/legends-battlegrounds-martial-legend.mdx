<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combo Execution">

To excel with **legends battlegrounds martial legend**, you must move beyond button mashing. The following steps outline the most reliable damage routes used by top-ranked players in the July 2026 competitive season.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The M1 Confirmed Starter">
    Approach the opponent with a side-dash to avoid their block. Land **3 M1 hits**, then immediately hold the Jump key while performing the 4th hit to trigger an **Uppercut**. This launches the opponent into the air, making them vulnerable to your next special move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Panther Extension">
    While the opponent is mid-air from the Uppercut, activate **Panther Combo (3)**. This multi-hit sequence keeps them suspended. Wait for the final kick of the Panther Combo to connect before preparing your next input.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Dragon Rush Catch">
    As the opponent begins to fall from the Panther Combo, use **Dragon Rush (1)**. Timing is critical here; if you use it too early, they may be too high to grab. If timed correctly, you will grab them mid-air and launch them again.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Tiger Palm Finisher">
    After the Dragon Rush knockback, perform a **Forward Dash (Q)** to close the distance. Land **3 more M1 hits**, followed by a **Downslam** (holding S/Down during the 4th M1). While they are on the ground, use **Tiger Palm (2)**. Since Tiger Palm hits grounded targets and breaks blocks, it is the perfect ender.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Evasive Check">
During Step 2 or 3, watch the opponent closely. If they use their **Ragdoll Cancel (Q)**, immediately stop your combo, hold **Block (F)**, and prepare to counter their retaliation.
</Callout>
