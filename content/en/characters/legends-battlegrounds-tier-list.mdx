### Shinji's Flashstep Reset Mechanic
Shinji features a unique passive called **Lightning Rage**. If you land a Nunchuck Flurry or Body Flicker while your Flashstep Fury is on cooldown, the cooldown is instantly refreshed. This allows Shinji players to stay aggressive and keep opponents in a constant state of pressure.
