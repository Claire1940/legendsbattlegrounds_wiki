<Section icon={Map} title="Advanced Tech and Combo Optimization">

To truly master **legends battlegrounds combos**, you must look beyond basic ability sequences and incorporate advanced "Tech." These are unintended or highly specific mechanical interactions that extend combos or reset cooldowns.

**Not Done Yet Tech**
This is the most critical high-level tech. By timing your inputs perfectly, you can reset the combo counter, allowing for theoretically infinite chains. This is often used with Martial Legend and Jun to push damage beyond the 100% mark, ensuring a kill even if the opponent has slight healing active.

**Tiger's Grasp Tech**
Specifically for Martial Legend, this involves waiting until the very last frame of a cooldown before initiating the move. This "buffers" the cooldown reduction for the next cycle, allowing you to use your heavy-hitting abilities twice in a single extended string.

**Miss Tech**
Commonly used by Jun players, Miss Tech involves intentionally missing the first part of an ability to trigger a secondary hit or a movement reset that catches the opponent off-guard during their recovery frames.
