To maximize your damage output, you must learn how to stack your moves using intermediate and advanced combo techniques. Standard attack strings are useful for basic damage, but integrating uppercuts and down slams allows you to extend your combos and keep the opponent in a state of hitstun for longer durations.

One of the most effective methods for extending combos is the front dash uppercut technique. By executing a front dash immediately after initiating an uppercut, you can cancel a portion of the animation recovery. This allows you to land a total of five basic strikes (M1s) instead of the standard four, significantly increasing your total damage output before the combo scaling limits take effect.
