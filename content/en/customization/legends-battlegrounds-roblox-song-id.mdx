<Section icon={Settings} title="How to Setup Your Song ID and Audio">

Setting up your **Legends Battlegrounds roblox song id** requires a few specific steps within the game menu. While the combat is fast-paced, the UI for customization is designed to be accessible once you have the correct gamepass.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Purchase the Kill Sound Pass">
    Navigate to the in-game shop or the Roblox game page. The **Kill Sound** gamepass is required to enable the ID input field. Without this pass, you cannot play custom audio upon eliminations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find a Valid Roblox Asset ID">
    Search the Roblox Creator Marketplace for "Audio." Copy the numerical ID found in the URL of the sound you want to use (e.g., `roblox.com/library/123456789/My-Sound`).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Input the ID in Settings">
    Open the Legends Battlegrounds customization menu. Locate the **Kill Sound** section and paste your numerical ID into the provided text box.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Test in Private Server">
    Use the **Private Server+** tools to quickly trigger an elimination and verify that your **Legends Battlegrounds roblox song id** is playing at the desired volume.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Audio Moderation">
Roblox has strict rules regarding audio IDs. If your chosen song ID is deleted by Roblox moderators, it will stop working in-game. Always keep a backup ID ready.
</Callout>
