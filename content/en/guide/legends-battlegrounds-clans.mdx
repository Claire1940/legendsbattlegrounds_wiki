<Section icon={Swords} title="Clan Wars and Territory Control Mechanics">

The heart of the **legends battlegrounds clans** experience is the Clan War system. In 2026, this has evolved into a persistent "Territory Control" map where clans fight for dominance over specific landmarks. Capturing these locations provides passive income in the form of Battle Coins and exclusive crafting materials.

Territory wars are typically scheduled events, occurring twice weekly. During these windows, the map becomes a massive battlefield where coordination is more important than individual skill. The "Friends are Strength" clan, for example, uses a "Phalanx" strategy, where tank-class heroes protect high-damage glass cannons during objective captures.

| Landmark Type | Capture Reward | Defense Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Ancient Forge** | Weapon Upgrade Stones | High |
| **The Grand Arena** | Double XP Tokens | Moderate |
| **Resource Outpost** | Daily Battle Coins | Low |
| **Capital Citadel** | Unique Clan Title | Extreme |

<Callout type="info" title="War Mechanics">
Territory control is calculated every Sunday at 00:00 UTC. The clan holding the most territory receives the "Dominator" buff for the following week, increasing all resource gains by 15%.
</Callout>

Tactical roles within these wars are strictly defined. Leading clans assign members to specific squads:
- **Vanguard:** Primary engagement and frontline pressure.
- **Infiltrators:** Flanking and capturing backline objectives.
- **Support:** Providing heals, shields, and crowd control.
- **Command:** Shot-calling and monitoring the mini-map for enemy movements.

</Section>
