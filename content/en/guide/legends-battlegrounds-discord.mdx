<Section icon={Target} title="Verification and Setup Process">

To access the full suite of features in the **legends battlegrounds discord**, you must complete the verification process. This connects your Discord identity with your Roblox account, allowing the server to assign roles based on your in-game achievements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Verify Channel">
    Find the channel named #verify or #get-started. This is usually the only channel visible to new members.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Verification Bot">
    Click the link provided by the bot (usually Bloxlink or RoVer). You will be redirected to a secure website to log in with your Roblox credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confirm Identity">
    Follow the prompts on the verification site. You may be asked to join a specific "verification game" or add a code to your Roblox profile description.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Update Roles">
    Return to Discord and type the command `/verify` or click the "Update Roles" button. The bot will sync your data and unlock the rest of the server.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Role Type | Requirement | Perk/Benefit |
