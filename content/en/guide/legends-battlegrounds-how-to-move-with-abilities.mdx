<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Movement and I-Frames">

Learning **legends battlegrounds how to move with abilities** isn't just about offense. Using abilities defensively can save your life when caught in a corner or trapped in a high-damage ultimate. Certain abilities grant "Invincibility Frames" (I-frames), allowing you to pass through attacks unharmed.

- **Dashing through Projectiles**: Timing your dash ability to move *through* an incoming fireball rather than away from it.
- **Vertical Escapes**: Using Jun's Uppercut to soar above ground-level ultimates like "Earthquake" or "Fire Wall."
- **Baiting Skills**: Moving toward an enemy with a movement ability and then immediately dashing backward to bait their counter-move.
