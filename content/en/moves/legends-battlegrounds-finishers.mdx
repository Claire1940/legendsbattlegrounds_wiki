<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How Finishers Work in Legends Battlegrounds">

Securing a victory in the Roblox arena is not just about reducing your opponent's health bar to zero; it is about doing it with style. In this fast-paced stickman PvP fighter, **legends battlegrounds finishers** serve as the ultimate cinematic exclamation point to a hard-fought duel. These animations trigger automatically when a player lands the final blow of a round or match using a qualifying skill. 

Rather than standard ragdoll physics, a successful finisher locks both players into a highly polished, camera-tracked martial arts sequence. This mechanic highlights the game's signature visual style—minimalist black stick figures clashing amidst vibrant energy rings and high-contrast impact streaks.

Understanding which moves trigger these animations is vital for competitive players. While standard light attack chains (M1s) simply knock an opponent away at the end of their health bar, special moves like Jun's Fang Dance or Glimpse of Power pull the camera in close, ensuring your opponent witnesses their defeat in dramatic fashion.

<Callout type="tip" title="Finisher Execution Rule">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Awakening and Combined Finishers">

When a fighter fills their awakening bar and presses **G**, they unlock an entirely new tier of cinematic devastation. Jun's awakening mode, **No More Games**, replaces his base kit with sword-based strikes and multi-angle teleport barrages. The absolute pinnacle of visual flair in the game is the combined awakening finisher, which merges two separate awakening skills into a singular, highly destructive sequence.

| Character | Awakening Mode | Primary Finisher | Combined Variant Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jun** | No More Games | Savage Fangs | Press **4 (One Inch Punch)** during **3 (Piercing Tempest)** |
| **Martial Legend** | Dragon's Fury | Draconic Honor | Standard alignment down a clear lane |
| **Shinji** | Warrior's Grief | Draco Meteor | Direct landing impact on low-health targets |

Executing Jun's combined ending requires precise timing and resource management. Follow these steps to perform the ultimate combined finisher in your next match:

  <Step num="1" title="Build the Awakening Meter">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait the Opponent's Evasive">
    Ensure your opponent has spent their Q ragdoll escape. Attempting a combined finisher while they have defensive options is highly risky.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate No More Games">
    Create space with a knockback or side-dash, then press **G** to safely activate your awakening without being interrupted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Piercing Tempest Hit">
    Press **3** to throw Jun's ranged knives. The opening hit must connect to initiate the teleporting sword barrage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Buffer the One Inch Punch Ending">

  </Step>
</Steps>
