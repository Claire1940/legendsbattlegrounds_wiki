<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Glimpse Cascade Combo Routes">

Executing a successful combo with Jun requires precise timing and an understanding of the opponent's cooldowns. Because Glimpse of Power is unblockable, it is often the move that finishes a fight after the opponent has already spent their Ragdoll Cancel.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Neutral Starter">
    Begin with a standard Forward Dash into a 3rd M1 hit. Instead of finishing the chain, use an **Uppercut** or a **Downslam** to force the opponent into a vulnerable state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Glimpse Confirmation">
    Immediately trigger **Glimpse of Power (1)**. Because it is unblockable, even if the opponent recovers and tries to block, the drive will connect and start the cinematic sequence.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Variant Transition">
    While Jun is performing the strikes of Glimpse of Power, press **Virgule Cascade (4)**. This activates the combined variant, replacing the standard ending with a high-damage dagger barrage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Follow-up Chase">
    As the Glimpse Cascade sequence ends, the target will be launched. Use a **Side Dash** to line up a **Forward Dash** and catch the landing with another M1 string or **Wolf Fang Flash**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Combo Type | Sequence | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Ground** | 3 M1 > Downslam > Fang Dance > Dash > Glimpse | Easy |
| **Glimpse Variant** | 3 M1 > Glimpse > Virgule Cascade (during 1) | Medium |
| **Wall Carry** | 3 M1 > Downslam > Wall Combo > Airborne Virgule | Hard |

<Callout type="warning" title="Evasive Check">
If your opponent uses their **Ragdoll Cancel (Q)** during the initial M1 string or the downslam, do not commit to Glimpse of Power. Instead, reset to a blocking stance (F) and wait for them to waste their next move.
</Callout>
