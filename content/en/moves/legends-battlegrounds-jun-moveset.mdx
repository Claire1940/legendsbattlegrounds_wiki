<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Special Abilities and the 2nd Ultimate">

The core of the **legends battlegrounds jun moveset** lies in his four primary abilities and his devastating Ultimates. Each move is designed to flow into the next, creating a rhythmic combat experience that rewards players who can maintain pressure.

**Move 1: Palm Thrust**
This is your primary guard-break tool. It has a short wind-up but deals significant stamina damage. If the opponent is blocking, this will likely stagger them, opening them up for a full combo.

**Move 2: Rising Knee**
A classic launcher. This move sends opponents into the air, allowing for aerial follow-ups. In the current 2026 build, the hitbox for Rising Knee has been slightly widened to make it more reliable in laggy conditions.

**Move 3: Counter Stance**
Jun enters a brief defensive state. If struck, he immediately teleports behind the attacker and delivers a stunning blow. This is his most iconic defensive tool, accompanied by the "Too easy" voice line.

**Move 4: Dragon Sweep**
A low-hitting kick that ignores most high-profile blocks. It's excellent for catching opponents who are waiting for a Palm Thrust.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Matchups and Strategic Counter-Play">

Knowing when to pick Jun is just as important as knowing how to play him. The **legends battlegrounds jun moveset** thrives against characters with slow animations but struggles against those with high mobility or long-range zoning capabilities.

| Opponent Type | Difficulty | Key Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zoners/Ranged** | Hard | Use dash-cancels and Counter Stance to close gaps. |
| **Heavies/Tanks** | Easy | Out-speed their slow M1s and use Dragon Sweep often. |
| **Speedsters** | Medium | Focus on parrying and wait for them to exhaust stamina. |
| **Mirror Match** | Skill-Based | The player who lands the first Counter Stance usually wins. |

Jun's "True Kung Fu" passive allows him to regenerate stamina 15% faster than other characters while in a neutral stance. This means you should avoid constant dashing; instead, move purposefully and let your stamina recover between combo attempts.

<Callout type="info" title="Matchup Secret">
Against ranged users, you can actually parry certain projectiles with your Counter Stance, which will still trigger the teleport follow-up if you are within a certain range.
</Callout>
