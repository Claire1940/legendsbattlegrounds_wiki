<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Matchups and Defensive Play">

Using the **legends battlegrounds jun one inch punch** effectively requires understanding how to bait your opponent into a vulnerable state. Because the move is tied to awakening, your opponent will likely play more defensively once they see Jun draw his sword. You must use your mobility to force them into making a mistake.

**Matchup Strategies:**

- **Against Martial Legend**: Watch for his Triple Kick Counter. If you trigger his counter, you waste your awakening time. Use Phantom Sever to stay behind him and bait the counter before committing to your Piercing Tempest.
- **Against Shinji**: Shinji is faster than Jun. He will try to use Flashstep Fury to kiting you. Use the raw version of One Inch Punch to catch his aggressive rushes. If he uses his lightning movement, wait for the landing lag to throw your knives.
- **Against Jun (Mirror)**: The first person to spend their evasive (Q) usually loses. Save your ragdoll cancel for their Glimpse Cascade. If you can force the other Jun to use Q, your Piercing Tempest into One Inch Punch becomes a guaranteed kill.

| Opponent | Jun's Advantage | Opponent's Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Martial Legend** | Better wall pressure, higher burst | Stable counter, safer neutral |
| **Shinji** | Unblockable variants, cinematic damage | Superior mobility, cooldown resets |
| **Jun** | Technical skill determines the winner | High damage punishes mistakes |

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Positioning">
Always try to activate your awakening near the center of the arena. If you are pushed into a corner, your Piercing Tempest knives might hit map geometry instead of the player, wasting your most powerful cooldown.
</Callout>
