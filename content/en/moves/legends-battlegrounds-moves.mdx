<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Tips and Anti-Teaming">

Playing Legends Battlegrounds in 2026 often involves dealing with "teamers" (multiple players attacking one). Fortunately, the game includes specific mechanics to help solo players survive and thrive in these situations.

**Anti-Team Mechanic:**
Jun features a unique anti-team feature. When the game detects you are being targeted by multiple players, you can summon a clone that automatically attacks one of the teamers. This allows you to focus your main **legends battlegrounds moves** on a single target without constant interruption.

**Advanced Combo Extension:**
The "Wolf Fang Flash Method" is a high-level technique for extending combos. By facing sideways (roughly 30 degrees) during the backflip part of the move, you can use the dash to move right next to your opponent without actually hitting them with the dash punch. This prevents them from blocking or countering while allowing you to continue your M1 string.

<Callout type="tip" title="Combo Consistency">
The 30-degree angle method is much more reliable than trying to miss the dash punch head-on. Practice this in the training area to master the spacing.
</Callout>
