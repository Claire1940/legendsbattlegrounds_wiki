<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Path to Obliteration: Awakening Mechanics">

The "Awakening" system is where the true power of each character is unleashed. By dealing and receiving damage, players fill a meter that, once full, allows for a transformation (G). In this state, your standard moveset is replaced by four "Obliteration-class" abilities that can end a fight in seconds.

For example, Jun's awakening, **No More Games**, replaces his dagger and martial arts with a sword-based kit. His "Piercing Tempest" move is a ranged knife spread that transitions into a teleport barrage, effectively trapping the opponent in a cinematic sequence of high-damage strikes.

| Character | Awakening Name | Key Move | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Martial Legend** | Dragon's Fury | Draconic Honor | Summons a charging dragon to carry targets. |
| **Jun** | No More Games | Savage Fangs | High-damage single-target cinematic burst. |
| **Shinji** | Warrior's Grief | Draco Meteor | Massive area-of-effect lightning impact. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Awakening Vulnerability">
The transformation animation has a brief startup. If you activate Awakening while an opponent is right on top of you, they can interrupt the process. Always create space with a knockback or a dash before pressing G.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Combo Routes for Jun">

Jun is the pinnacle of technical combat in Legends Battlegrounds. His kit relies on "variants"—moves that change properties based on when they are used. Mastering the **Glimpse Cascade** route is essential for any Jun player looking to dominate the 2026 meta.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Opening Confirm">
    Start with a **3 M1 string** followed by a **Downslam**. This forces the opponent to either spend their evasive (Q) or take the full force of your next move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Fang Dance Bridge">
    Immediately trigger **Fang Dance**. This rush attack carries the opponent across the map. As the animation ends, perform a **Forward Dash** to stay in range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Variant Transition">
    Land another **3 M1 string**, then activate **Glimpse of Power**. During the Glimpse animation, press **4 (Virgule Cascade)** to trigger the combined **Glimpse Cascade** variant for maximum damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Finisher">
    If the opponent is low on health, end the sequence with **Wolf Fang Flash**. If they are near a wall, use the **Wall Combo** to launch them for an aerial Virgule Cascade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Evasive Checking">
Before committing to your longest combo, watch the opponent's character model. If they use their ragdoll cancel early, you are free to use your highest-damage cooldowns without fear of them escaping mid-animation.
</Callout>

</Section>
