<Section icon={Shield} title="Countering Shinji: Defensive Tactics">

Despite the raw power of the **legends battlegrounds shinji ult**, the character has significant weaknesses that savvy players can exploit. Most of Shinji's moves are highly telegraphed and, more importantly, blockable.

**How to Defeat Shinji:**
1.  **Hold Block**: Unlike characters with many unblockable "red" moves, Shinji relies on landing his base kit to build meter. Holding block negates Nunchuck Flurry and Body Flicker entirely.
2.  **Punish Body Flicker**: After Shinji uses Body Flicker, there is a small delay. If you blocked the move, you can immediately start an M1 chain or use a grab move to punish him.
3.  **Ragdoll Cancel**: Save your ragdoll cancel for Nunchuck Flurry. If you escape the flurry, Shinji doesn't get his cooldown reset for Flashstep Fury, breaking his offensive rhythm.

| Shinji Move | Counter Action | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nunchuck Flurry** | Block or Ragdoll Cancel | Low |
| **Body Flicker** | Block and M1 Startup | Medium |
| **Flashstep Fury** | Watch for indicator and block | Medium |
| **Iron Onslaught** | Dodge lunge or hit before blue flash | High |

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanics Note">
Shinji's **Lightning Rage** passive is indicated by a blue bar at the bottom of the Flashstep Fury icon. When this bar is filling (takes about 50 seconds), he cannot reset his dash, making him significantly less mobile.
</Callout>
