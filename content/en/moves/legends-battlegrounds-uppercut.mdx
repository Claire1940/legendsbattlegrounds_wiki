<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Counter-Play and Combo Types">

Understanding the **legends battlegrounds uppercut** also means knowing how to defend against it. Combat in this game is categorized into three distinct combo types. Recognizing which one your opponent is attempting will dictate your defensive strategy.

1.  **True Combos**: These are inescapable sequences where the hit-stun duration exceeds the startup of the next move. If you are hit by an uppercut in a True Combo, your only option is to use an Evasive.
2.  **Regular Combos**: These have small gaps. If an opponent mistimes their **legends battlegrounds uppercut**, you can use a "Dash Cancel" to escape the sequence without wasting your Evasive meter.
3.  **One Shot Combos**: These are specialized strings designed to take a player from 100% to 0% HP. They almost always utilize a jump-buffered uppercut to maximize air time.

| Combo Type | Key Characteristic | Best Defense |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **True Combo** | No gaps between hits | Evasive (Blue Meter) |
| **Regular Combo** | Escapable via timing | Dash Cancel / Side Dash |
| **One Shot** | Kills from full HP | Don't get hit by the opener |
| **Infinite** | Uses "Not Done Yet" Tech | Perfect Evasive timing |

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Mechanic">
A "Dash Cancel" is performed by inputting a dash command during the very first frame of a non-true combo gap. This is the hallmark of a high-skill player in *Legends Battlegrounds*.
</Callout>
