<Section icon={Swords} title="Legends Battlegrounds Leaks: Combat Overhaul">

The core gameplay loop of the game is preparing for a monumental shift. Developer Zale has redesigned how the game registers attacks, transitioning the system from server-side verification to client-side registration. This structural change means that high latency or ping spikes should no longer disrupt your combo execution or cause attacks to drop mid-fight. Attacks will register based on what you see on your screen rather than waiting for server confirmation, offering a much more responsive and reliable combat experience.

In addition to the underlying netcode improvements, the update introduces clean and unique dash cooldown indicators. These visual gauges are designed to help players manage their mobility options during high-intensity skirmishes without cluttering the screen.
