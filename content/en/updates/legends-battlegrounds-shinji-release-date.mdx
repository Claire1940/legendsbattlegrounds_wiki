<Section icon={Swords} title="Shinji Base Moveset and Combat Analysis">

Currently accessible via the Early Access Gamepass, Shinji is the third playable legend in the arena. Based on the iconic character from Jhanzou's Combat Gods animation series, Shinji brings lightning-fast martial arts and nunchuck-based combos to the battlefield. He stands out as the only character in the game to feature a unique 5-hit M1 string, structured as four distinct inputs, with the third input delivering two rapid nunchuck strikes.

To maximize Shinji's potential, players must understand the damage values, cooldowns, and utility of his base moveset.

| Attack | Damage | Cooldown | Utility & Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **M1 String** | **15%** | None | Right hook, left jab, double nunchuck strike, and a spinning kick. |
| **Nunchuck Flurry** | **17%** | 30 Seconds | Launches opponents upward. Can hit ragdolled and multiple targets. |
| **Body Flicker** | **13%** | 20 Seconds | Blitzes forward with electric damage. Excellent for mobility and escapes. |
| **Wall Combo** | **16%** | Contextual | Pins opponent to a wall, delivering a high-speed electrical punch barrage. |

When transitioning into his awakened state, Shinji gains access to devastating ultimate abilities that can easily turn the tide of any engagement. These cinematic attacks deal massive damage but require careful positioning to execute successfully.

| Ultimate Move | Damage | Cooldown | Mechanics & Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warrior's Grief** | **29%** | 100% Ult Bar | Cutscene move with a minor stun wave. Grants temporary invincibility. |
| **Iron Onslaught** | **83%** | 35 Seconds | High-damage multi-stage teleport attack. Cancelable before eyes flash blue. |
| **Draco Meteor** | **Variable** | Awakening | Reference to Gojo's honored one quote. Massive area-of-effect damage. |

One of the most unique aspects of Shinji's ultimate moveset is the cinematic styling. **Warrior's Grief** features a dramatic cavern sequence accompanied by narration identical to Madara Uchiha's famous speech from Naruto. Meanwhile, **Iron Onslaught** plays Little V's cover of Red Like Roses 2, a energetic rock theme that fits the high-speed teleporting punches perfectly.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Passives and Strategic Playstyle">

Shinji's core combat loop revolves around his unique passive, **Lightning Rage**. This mechanic rewards aggressive play and smart cooldown management, allowing skilled players to maintain relentless pressure on their opponents.

When Shinji's fourth ability, **Flashstep Fury**, is on cooldown, successfully landing either **Nunchuck Flurry** or **Body Flicker** triggers the Lightning Rage passive. This fills a blue bar at the bottom of the move icon, taking approximately 50 seconds to fully reset the cooldown of Flashstep Fury. This mechanic ensures that Shinji can constantly cycle his high-mobility tools to chase down fleeing opponents or escape tight situations.

To play Shinji effectively, you must balance your high-speed rushes with defensive patience. Because his moves do not grant invincibility frames (I-frames), throwing out attacks recklessly will result in easy parries or blocks from your opponent.

To dominate the battlegrounds with Shinji, you must master his combo flow and passive resets. Follow this step-by-step sequence to maximize your damage output and maintain pressure.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate and Launch">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute Body Flicker">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Predict and Chase">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger Lightning Rage">
